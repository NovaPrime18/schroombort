Battery powered soundboard with 36 mechanical buttons and up to 3 different configurations. 


Configurations:
1. Soundboard, press a button and a sound from the SD-card plays!
2. Macro-pad for your computer, connect it via USB - C and configure what macro's do what.
3. Calculator mode, just a basic calculator.


Specs:

    Can charge from any 5V charger, up to 2 amps. 
    Fully dynamic power-path management, can run entirely from USB, Battery or a healthy mix of both.


    36 configurable mechanical buttons
    stereo speakers
    atxmega256A3U microcontroller
    Vacuum Fluorescent Display
